Privacy Policy

S7 Image & Data Grabber does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

The application operates entirely on the user's local device and processes files and images selected by the user.

No user data is transmitted to external servers.

No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used.

If you have questions regarding this policy, you may contact the developer via the Microsoft Store listing.
